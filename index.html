About me

I'm Anwar Khan, a computational biologist. I work as an ML Engineer/ Bioinformatician at VIB-KU Leuven Center for Cancer Biology, Belgium. I earned my master's in Biomedical Science and Engineering, specializing in Data Science in Oncology and Bioinformatics, from Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology, South Korea. My research focuses on developing ML tools for high-throughput single-cell RNA-seq data analysis, spatial transcriptomics, gene regulatory networks, drug discovery, digital pathology, and NLP.
